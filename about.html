About the Artist

Philosophy, Journey & Vision

Biography

The Beginning

Pramod Kurlekar's artistic journey began in the vibrant landscapes of India, where he was captivated by the interplay of light and shadow in nature. His formal training in classical realism provided the foundation for his meticulous technique.

Evolution

Over two decades, his style has evolved from strict realism to a more evocative, conceptual approach. He blends the precision of the old masters with contemporary themes of human emotion and environmental symbiosis.

Art Works Context

Vision & Technique

Every stroke in Kurlekar's work is intentional. He utilizes a multi-layered glazing technique to achieve depth and luminosity that seems to glow from within the canvas. His "Contextual Realism" series explores the hidden narratives behind everyday objects and portraits.
