The Artist

Mangesh Shinde is a master of contemporary realism, blending classical techniques with modern conceptual themes. His work explores the delicate balance between the human experience and the natural world.

With pieces held in private collections globally, Shinde continues to push the boundaries of artistic expression.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see."
